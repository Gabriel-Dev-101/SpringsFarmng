About Us

SpringsFarm.ng Ltd. Springs Farms is an indigenous agricultural business based in Edo State, our branches spreads across 6 state in Nigeria as our influence covers the boarders of Africa. We are producers of Palm Oil (PMO) and Palm Kernel Oil (PKO).

Our Vision

At SpringsFarm.ng Ltd, our Vision is to be a key player in palm oil and palm kernel oil production, renowned for environmental excellence, social responsibility, and customer satisfaction, while promoting innovation and industrialization locally and globally.

Our Mission

At SpringsFarm.ng Ltd, our mission is to provide quality unadulterated products and services to all our customers and partners globally, sustaining economic progression, innovation, and industrialization.
